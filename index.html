About Me

I am a 5th-year BS-MS student in Physics at IISER Bhopal, India. My academic interests include theoretical high-energy physics, quantum field theory, quantum gravity, and cosmology. I come from Bihar, a state in eastern India known for its rich history and deep philosophical traditions. When I’m not working on a physics problem, I enjoy reading philosophical books, noticing things around me, and playing basketball and table tennis. I’m also fascinated by how ideas evolve—whether in science, society, or individual minds.
